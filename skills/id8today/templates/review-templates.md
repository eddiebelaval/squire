# Review Templates

## Purpose

Regular reviews keep the system working. These templates support daily, weekly, and monthly review rituals.

---

## Daily Review (5 minutes)

### Morning Check-In

```markdown
## Morning Check: {date}

### How am I starting?
- **Sleep:** {Good/OK/Poor}
- **Energy:** {1-10}
- **Mood:** {word or phrase}

### What's already on my plate?
- {existing commitments}
- {meetings}
- {deadlines}

### Today's Top 3
1. {most important}
2. {second}
3. {third}

### First action
**Start with:** {specific task}
**At:** {time}

### Method for today
**Using:** {method name}
**Why:** {fits today's context}

---

Ready to begin.
```

```markdown
## Day Close: {date}

### What got done?

- [x] {completed}

- {blocker}

### Day rating

**Satisfaction:** {1-5}

{insight from today}

---

```

## Quick Reference: Review Schedule

| Review | When | Duration | Template |
|--------|------|----------|----------|
| Morning | Daily, start of work | 5 min | Morning Check-In |
| Evening | Daily, end of work | 5 min | Evening Close |
| Weekly | Friday/Sunday | 15-30 min | Friday Review |
| Monthly | Last day of month | 30-60 min | End of Month |
| Quarterly | End of quarter | 1-2 hours | End of Quarter |
